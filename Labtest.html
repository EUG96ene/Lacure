Lab Tests

Our experienced team of professionals utilizes cutting-edge equipment and follows strict quality control protocols to ensure the accuracy and reliability of every test.

Complete Blood Count

Is a test that measures the levels of red blood cells, white blood cells, and platelets in the blood. It is used to diagnose a wide range of medical conditions.

Urinalysis

Is a test that examines a urine sample for various compounds, including protein, glucose, and blood cells. It can be used to diagnose urinary tract infections, kidney problems.

Connect

Lipid Profile

Is a blood test that measures the levels of cholesterol and triglycerides in the blood. It is used to monitor the effectiveness of cholesterol-lowering medications.

Connect

Glucose Tolerance Test

Is a test that measures how quickly the body processes glucose. It is used to diagnose diabetes and to monitor blood sugar levels in people with diabetes.

Connect

Bilirubin Blood Test

Is a diagnostic test that measures the levels of bilirubin in the blood. Bilirubin is a yellow pigment that is produced when red blood cells break down.

Connect

Electrolyte Panel

Is a test that measures the levels of various electrolytes, such as sodium, potassium, and calcium. It is used to diagnose and monitor a wide range of medical conditions.
