faq

The most common questions

What is online doctor Consultation?


Online doctor consultation or online medical consultation is a method to connect patients and doctors virtually. It is a convenient and easy way to get online doctor advice using doctor apps or telemedicine apps or platforms, and the internet.

How do I start online consultation with doctors on Lacure?


Starting an online doctor consultation is very simple on Lacure. Follow these 4 simple steps: • Choose your health concern • Connect with a doctor within 2 minutes • Ask your queries to the doctor via audio or video call • Get a valid online doctor prescription and a 3-day free online doctor consultation

Are your online doctors qualified?


We follow a strict verification process for every doctor providing online medical services on Lacure. Our team manually verifies necessary documents, registrations, and certifications for every doctor.

Are there any special instructions or preparations needed before undergoing certain tests?


Some tests may require specific preparations, such as fasting for a certain duration before blood work or avoiding certain medications prior to the test. Our team will provide you with detailed instructions and any necessary preparations when you schedule your test to ensure accurate results.

Is online doctor consultation safe and secured on Lacure?


The privacy of our patients is critical to us, and thus, we are compliant with industry standards like ISO 27001. Rest assured that your online consultation with a doctor is completely safe and secured with 256-bit encryption.

What happens if I don’t get a response from a doctor?


In the unlikely event that an online doctor does not respond, you will be entitled to a 100% refund.

Are your laboratory technicians certified and experienced in performing the tests?


Yes, our laboratory technicians are certified professionals with extensive experience in their respective areas of expertise. They undergo rigorous training and continuously update their knowledge to stay abreast of the latest advancements in laboratory diagnostics. Your tests are handled by skilled experts dedicated to delivering accurate and precise results.

What is the cost associated with laboratory testing, and do you accept insurance?


The cost of laboratory testing can vary depending on the specific tests required. We accept most major insurance plans, and coverage for laboratory tests may depend on your insurance policy. We recommend contacting your insurance provider to understand the coverage details. For uninsured individuals, we offer competitive pricing options and strive to make our services accessible to all.
