Data security for patients

Patients

At Lacure Medicare, your data is strictly confidential. Our team has no access to view your information, and we only send messages with your consent. You have the option to opt out of promotional messages, and we never share data with third parties.

Data security for Doctors

Doctors

At Lacure Medicare, we have no access to your practice data and never share it with third parties. Doctors have complete control over patient communication. We adhere to strict data policies to prioritize user privacy and security.

Secure Healthcare Platform.

Sole Data Ownership.

Lacure Medicare ensures data security and privacy across all products. Walk-in patients are not sent promotions, data is encrypted and backed up, and selling data is prohibited. Doctors' and patients' data are kept separate, and strict privacy measures are implemented. Two-factor authentication and remote logout enhance data protection.
